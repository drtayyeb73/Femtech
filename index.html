Pelvic Floor Exercises

Guided Exercises

Kegel Exercises

Strengthen your pelvic floor muscles to improve bladder control and sexual health.

Step 1: Identify the right muscles by trying to stop urination midstream.

Step 2: Tighten these muscles for 5 seconds, then relax for 5 seconds.

Step 3: Repeat 10-15 times, 3 times daily.

Bridge Pose

Strengthens glutes, lower back, and pelvic floor muscles.

Step 1: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.

Step 2: Lift your hips toward the ceiling while squeezing your glutes.

Step 3: Hold for 10 seconds, then slowly lower down. Repeat 10 times.

Deep Breathing

Supports pelvic floor relaxation and stress reduction through breath control.

Step 1: Sit or lie comfortably with one hand on your lower belly.

Step 2: Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 seconds, expanding the belly.

Step 3: Exhale gently for 6 seconds and relax the pelvic floor. Repeat 10 cycles.

About Application

AI-Driven Women's Health and Femtech Hub is a mobile-first wellness application. It combines cycle intelligence, symptom support, menopause workflows, and sensor-assisted exercise guidance in one local-first app.

Feature 1: Personalized Cycle and Fertility Tracking

  • Inputs captured: cycle length, last period date, symptoms, mood, and energy level.
  • Predictions shown: Next Period, Fertile Window, and Ovulation Day.
  • Calendar view marks cycle phases and stores daily entries locally.
  • Day details panel combines cycle logs with menopause, hydration, and supplement records for that date.

Feature 2: AI Symptom Checker (Rule-Based)

  • Users can select predefined symptoms and add free-text symptoms.
  • Engine uses weighted rule logic for summary, self-care, doctor priority, and treatment guidance text.
  • Cycle phase and menopause mode influence recommendation language.
  • Medical disclaimer is explicit: this is wellness guidance, not diagnosis.

Feature 3: Menopause Support Mode

  • Tracks Hot Flashes, Sleep Quality, Mood Changes, and Brain Fog severity.
  • Generates personalized menopause tips based on severity combinations.
  • Mode toggle updates app context between reproductive and menopause workflows.

Feature 4: Pelvic Floor Exercises with Sensor Support

  • Exercises: Kegels, Bridge Pose, and Deep Breathing.
  • Motion support includes permission handling, live motion signal, and calibration per exercise.
  • Distinct visual systems are implemented for each exercise with unique color palette and animation behavior.
  • Fallback manual guidance remains available when device sensors are unsupported or permission is denied.

Feature 5: Hydration and Supplements Integration

  • Hydration tracker records cups per day with Add/Reset controls.
  • Daily supplement logging supports Folic Acid, Magnesium, Iron, Omega-3, and Vitamin D.
  • Wellness Signals (Implemented) uses hydration + supplement logs in recommendation output.
  • Examples: low hydration warning, iron support hint for heavy bleeding/fatigue patterns, magnesium hint for mood/luteal context.

Feature 6: Nutrition and BMR

  • BMR calculator uses age, height, weight, and activity level.
  • Outputs BMR and daily calorie target.
  • Phase-aware nutrition tip updates based on detected cycle phase.

Feature 7: Trends Dashboard

  • Metrics available: Energy Level, Symptom Count, Hydration (Cups), Supplements Taken.
  • Graph is optimized for readability with metric-specific colors, gradients, improved tooltips, and mobile-friendly axes.
  • Supplements metric tooltip shows supplement names logged for each day.

Feature 8: Community Forum

  • Local topic-based community posting (title + content) with topic creation support.
  • Optional external forum integration via URL with fallback open-in-new-tab behavior.

Feature 9: Privacy and Security

  • All core app data persists locally in localStorage.
  • Clear All Data button removes local app records and resets state.
  • Multiple render paths were hardened to reduce XSS risk by avoiding unsafe HTML injection.

Feature 10: Provider PDF Report Export

  • Export Provider Report (PDF) button compiles key health logs into a clinician-friendly summary.
  • Includes cycle/fertility overview, 30-day trends summary, symptom patterns, menopause scores, hydration, and supplements.
  • Includes 14-day daily log table for quick consultation review.
  • Uses print-ready layout so users can save as PDF from Android/Chrome print dialog.

Safety Notice

This application is for education and wellness support only. It is not a substitute for professional diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care.
